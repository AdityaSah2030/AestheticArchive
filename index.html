About Me

Hello! I'm Aditya Sah, a tech enthusiast and passionate photographer based in Kolkata. Photography is my creative outlet - a way to capture moments and emotions that might otherwise be lost to time.

Through my lens, I seek to find beauty in everyday scenes and freeze emotions in their most authentic form. My photography is an extension of how I see the world - full of stories waiting to be told.

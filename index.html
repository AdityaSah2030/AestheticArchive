About Me

Hi, I’m Aditya Sah, a tech enthusiast and photographer based in Kolkata.

Photography is a way to slow down and notice things that usually go unseen. Simple moments stand out the most, like sunlight on old buildings, quiet evening streets, or expressions that feel real and unfiltered.

Each photo is lightly edited, just enough to enhance what was already there without losing its natural feel.

No high end camera or expensive gear involved. Most of these are simple mobile shots, taken to keep the passion alive and stay connected to the process.

The focus is not on perfection, but on capturing moments as they are. Small, honest frames that might otherwise pass by unnoticed.

Welcome to my world, one frame at a time.
